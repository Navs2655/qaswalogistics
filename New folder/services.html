Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

We provide our services across the UK, with a focus on major cities including London, Manchester, Birmingham, and Edinburgh. We also offer international moving services to selected European countries.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for larger moves or during peak seasons. However, we also offer last-minute and same-day services subject to availability.

Are you insured?

Yes, all our services are fully insured. We have comprehensive coverage for goods in transit and public liability insurance to ensure your peace of mind throughout the moving process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. A deposit may be required at the time of booking, with the balance payable upon completion of the service.
