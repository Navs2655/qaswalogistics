Our Vision

At Qaswa Logistics, we envision being the UK's most trusted name in moving and logistics services, known for our reliability, efficiency, and customer-first approach. We strive to revolutionize the relocation experience by combining modern technology with personalized service.

Our Journey

The Evolution of Qaswa Logistics

2016

The Beginning

Qaswa Logistics was founded with a single van and a vision to provide quality moving services to local clients in London.

2018

Expansion

With growing demand, we expanded our fleet and team to serve more areas across the UK, introducing professional packing services.

2020

Commercial Services

We launched our commercial relocation division, specializing in office moves and business logistics solutions.

2022

International Reach

We began offering international moving services, connecting the UK with Europe and beyond.

2025

Industry Leaders

Today, we're proud to be one of the most trusted names in the UK logistics industry, with thousands of satisfied customers and a comprehensive range of services.
